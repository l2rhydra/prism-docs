## Can I change the color of the inventory ?

Yes, you can customize the inventory colors by navigating to `\ox_inventory\config.lua` and modifying the color settings to match your preferences.

## Can I translate the inventory ?

Yes, the inventory supports multiple languages. You can set up translations by going to `ox_inventory\locales` and configuring your preferred locale settings.

## Does your version of ox_inventory matter ?

No, the version does not matter. This is a complete redesign that works as a plug-and-play solution, meaning it's compatible with any version of ox_inventory without requiring specific version requirements.
