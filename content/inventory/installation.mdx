## Installation

- **Ensure all dependencies are installed** [ [ox_lib](https://github.com/communityox/ox_lib/releases) [oxmysql](https://github.com/overextended/oxmysql) [MugShotBase64 (Optional)](https://github.com/BaziForYou/MugShotBase64) [On Game Build 6116 or higher](https://runtime.fivem.net/artifacts/fivem/build_server_windows/master/6116-8cd40bca9d038c43fc423e06afc3debe951c4511/server.7z) ]
- **Edit configuration as desired** (Optional)
- **Drag-and-drop our script** : Drag ox_inventory in to your `resources` directory and add ensure `ox_inventory` to your `server.cfg` after all dependencies.
- **Restart your server**.
