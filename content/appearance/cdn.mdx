# CDN

<div className='text-lg mt-2'
    style={{
        color: 'rgb(185, 198, 162)'
    }}
>This page explains how to upload your images to the Five Mange CDN.</div>

<Callout type="info">
  Why use a CDN? It provides players with a smoother experience on your server by eliminating the need to download all clothing images during login. Instead, the images are pre-downloaded through the CDN, allowing players to load in and create their character without any delays.
</Callout>

<Callout type="error">
  Make sure youâ€™ve completed all the steps for generating the images. If not, refer to the page that explains the process.
</Callout>

<Steps>
    ### Step 1

    Go to the [FiveManage](https://app.fivemanage.com/) website and create an account. Once your account is set up, create a team.

    ### Step 2

    Open your team, navigate to Storage, and click Upload Assets.

    <Image src='/fminfo.avif' alt='cdnstep2' width={1920} height={384} />

    ### Step 3

    Navigate to the `illenium-appearance\web\dist\clothing` folder, select all the clothing images, and begin uploading them.

    ### Step 4

    Once all clothing images have been successfully uploaded, click on any image and copy its ID.

    <Image src='/fminfo2.avif' alt='cdnstep4' width={1920} height={384} />

    ### Step 5

    Open the `illenium-appearance\shared\config.lua` file, enable the CDN option, and paste the ID.

    
    <Image src='/fminfo3.avif' alt='cdnstep4' width={1920} height={384} />

    ### Step 6

    Go to `illenium-appearance\web\dist\clothing`, delete all the images in the folder, restart your server, and enjoy.
</Steps>
