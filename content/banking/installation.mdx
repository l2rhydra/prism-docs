## Installation

- **Drag `prism_banking` in to your resources directory**
- **Edit configuration as desired** (Optional)
- **Drag-and-drop our script** : Drag `prism_banking` in to your `resources` directory and add `ensure prism_banking` to your `server.cfg`
- **Restart your server**.
